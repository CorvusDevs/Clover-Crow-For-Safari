Clover Crow brings the 4chan-X power-user toolkit to Safari. Open any board and it takes over: hover-preview quotes, inline image and video expansion, a thread watcher that badges unread counts, a draggable quick-reply box, regex filters, per-post translation, extended embeds, and a fully modernized catalog. It is not a Chrome extension ported across. It is built for Safari with native performance, no background processes, and zero data collection.

## Features

- **Quick Reply**, A draggable, resizable reply box with draft autosave and one-tap quoting. Reply without leaving your place in the thread.
- **Inline Image Expansion**, Click any thumbnail to expand the full image or video in place. Click again to collapse.
- **Thread Watcher**, Bookmark threads and watch unread counts badge on the favicon and the toolbar popup. Jump back to any watched thread in one tap.
- **Quote Threading**, Reddit-style indented replies with a nested view of who quoted whom, so long threads stay readable.
- **Quote Previews**, Hover a quote link to preview the linked post inline, with backlinks showing every reply.
- **Quick Filter**, One-click mute by name, tripcode, ID, image MD5, or filename. No settings dialog to dig through.
- **Translate Post**, A per-post translate button routed through Google, Bing, Yandex, or Papago.
- **Extended Embeds**, Inline players for YouTube, Vimeo, Streamable, Twitch, Imgur, Reddit, and Bitchute.
- **Modernized Catalog**, A cleaner, card-based catalog view with rounded thumbnails, hover states, and themed controls.
- **In-app language picker**, 33 languages with flag icons and native names. Defaults to your system locale; switch anytime from the picker in the top-right.
- **What's New on update**, The first launch after an update shows what changed, with "NEW" pills marking recently-added settings.
- **Multi-platform**, A native Safari Web Extension for macOS, iOS, and iPadOS, with settings that sync via iCloud when you sign in to Safari.

## Privacy

No account required. No sign-ups, no cloud tracking, no analytics. Your watched threads, filters, and preferences live entirely on your device. Clover Crow collects zero data. See the [full privacy policy](https://corvusdevs.github.io/Clover-Crow/privacy.html).

## About

Clover Crow is an independent project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by 4chan or its operators. The "4chan" name is used here descriptively to identify the platform this extension enhances.
